## Workflow

Execute steps in order. Go one by one. I don't mind if the process is slow, but good.

### Step 1 — behavioral_pset_torresval.pdf

- Open the folder presentation and read the two pdfs
- Do one by one not to waste tokens, even if slow.
- This contains the research proposals of my colleagues
- Make sure to understand the underlying problem, the model proposed and how the data needs to be collected.
- Make sure to know the main criticisms, main issues, proposal for improvement.q

### Step 2 - Inside the same folder, open the md document my_comments_present.md

It contains my comments for the presentations, what I find confusing and what could be improved.
Adapt your critiques to cover my points if you deem them important.

### Step 3- Create a short tex file peer_review.tex with the following

For each proposal

1. Introduction brief on the topic
2. Critiques - be brief to the point.
3. It is better to be brief and to the point, like a referee report, with fundamental. Brief points but well argued why that is an issue in the second sentence.
4. Propose ways to solution (I already do this)
5. End with positive note.
6. 1 pag max per review. brief.
